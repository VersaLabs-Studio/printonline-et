# PrintOnline Ethiopia — Version 3.7 Summary

**Date:** May 2026  

---

## What's New in This Update

Version 3.7 makes your website easier to manage. You can now update the homepage and other site content directly from the admin panel — no coding needed.

---

### 1. Homepage Hero Slideshow — Now Editable

The big rotating banner on your homepage can now be managed from the CMS.

- Add up to **4 slides** with your own images and text
- Change the "Order Now" button link per slide
- Turn slides on/off without deleting them
- Reorder slides by dragging them

**Where to find it:** CMS → Settings → Hero Slides

---

### 2. Special Deals Section — Now Editable

The "Special Deals & Promotions" section on the homepage is now fully editable.

- Add or change up to **3 deal cards**
- Set your own images, titles, descriptions, and links
- Choose badge colors (red, blue, green, etc.)
- Add countdown labels like "2 days left"

**Where to find it:** CMS → Settings → Deals

---

### 3. Testimonials — Now Editable

Customer reviews and testimonials can now be managed from the CMS.

- Add, edit, or remove customer testimonials
- Set names, companies, ratings (1-5 stars), and quotes
- Upload avatar images
- The homepage automatically rotates through active testimonials

**Where to find it:** CMS → Settings → Testimonials

---

### 4. Product Quantity Options — More Flexible

You now have more control over how customers choose quantities:

- **Manual entry:** Let customers type any quantity instead of picking from a dropdown
- **Quantity intervals:** Set increments (e.g., steps of 5, 10, or 25)
- **Custom presets:** Create your own dropdown options (e.g., 50, 100, 250, 500)

**Where to find it:** CMS → Products → [Product] → Edit → "Quantity Configuration"

---

### 5. Product Image Reordering

You can now drag and drop product images to reorder them:

- Click and drag any image to a new position
- The main display image updates automatically
- Changes save instantly

**Where to find it:** CMS → Products → [Product] → Image Manager

---

### 6. CMS Save Behavior — Improved

When you edit a product or category and click Save, you now stay on the edit page instead of being sent back to the list. This makes it easier to make multiple changes at once.

---

### 7. Cookie & Privacy Compliance

A cookie consent banner now appears when someone visits the site for the first time. Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Cookie Policy pages are now live.

- Cookie banner is non-intrusive (appears at the bottom)
- Once accepted, it won't show again
- Policy content is editable from the CMS

**Where to find it:** CMS → Settings → Policies | Homepage: Footer links

---

### 8. Contact Us Form

The "Unlock Exclusive Perks" newsletter section has been replaced with a "Get In Touch" contact form. Customers can now send inquiries directly through the homepage.

**Form fields:** Name, Email, Phone, Subject (dropdown), Message  
**What happens:** Messages are sent to the PrintOnline team email

---

### 9. Login with Google, Facebook, or TikTok

Customers can now sign in using their existing social media accounts:

| Provider | Type |
|----------|------|
| Google | Primary login option |
| Facebook | Secondary login option (To be integrated) |
| TikTok | Additional login option (to be integrated)|
| Email/Password | Still available as always |

**To enable:** You'll need to set up developer accounts with each provider. The team can assist with this.

---

### 10. Email Verification for New Accounts

New customers now receive an email to verify their account after registering. This reduces spam accounts and ensures valid contact information.

---

### 11. Real Password Recovery

The "Forgot Password" feature now sends real password reset emails instead of the simulated flow. Customers can securely reset their password if they forget it.

---

### 12. Homepage Categories — Now Editable

The "Shop by Category" section on the homepage now lets you control which categories are displayed and in what order.

- Choose which categories appear on the homepage
- Reorder categories by dragging them
- Toggle categories on/off without deleting them
- Product counts update automatically

**Where to find it:** CMS → Settings → Homepage Categories

---

### 13. Product Activation & Deactivation

You can now activate or deactivate products without deleting them. Deactivated products are hidden from the storefront but remain in your CMS for future reactivation.

- **Toggle switch** in the product list for instant activate/deactivate
- **Deactivated products** can be reactivated at any time
- **Permanent delete** is available only for deactivated products (with confirmation)
- Order history is preserved even if a product is permanently deleted

**Where to find it:** CMS → Products → Toggle switch or Actions menu

---

### 14. Footer Updated

- Removed the landline number (+251 116 68 69 40)
- All social media links now point to the contact page
- Privacy, Terms, and Cookie Policy links are now functional

---

## What Stays the Same

- **Checkout process** — unchanged
- **Order management** — unchanged
- **Product pages** — unchanged
- **Customer accounts** — unchanged
- **Pricing** — unchanged
- **Delivery fees** — unchanged
