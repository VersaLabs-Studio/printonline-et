## What's New in Version 3.5

### 3.1 Location-Based Delivery Pricing

**Category:** Core E-Commerce Enhancement  
**Impact Level:** High — Affects all customers selecting home delivery  
**User-Facing Change:** Delivery fees now vary based on your delivery address

#### What Changed

Previously, all deliveries within Addis Ababa were charged a flat delivery fee regardless of the customer's specific location. Starting with Version 3.5, delivery pricing is now calculated based on the **distance between your delivery address and the PrintOnline headquarters** located in Bole Sub City.

#### How It Works

When you proceed to checkout, the system automatically calculates your delivery fee based on the **sub-city** specified in your delivery address. The fee structure is designed to reflect the actual cost of delivery to your area, ensuring fair and transparent pricing for all customers.

#### Delivery Fee Structure

The delivery fee is determined by which of Addis Ababa's sub-cities your delivery address falls within. The system uses a tiered pricing model based on the approximate distance from our headquarters:

**Zone 1 — Headquarters Area (Bole)**
- Delivery Fee: 50 Birr
- Coverage: Bole Sub City and immediate surrounding areas
- Estimated Delivery: Standard timeline

**Zone 2 — Near Zone**
- Delivery Fee: 60 Birr
- Coverage: Kirkos, Arada
- Estimated Delivery: Standard timeline

**Zone 3 — Medium Zone**
- Delivery Fee: 70 Birr
- Coverage: Addis Ketema, Gulele, Yeka
- Estimated Delivery: Standard timeline

**Zone 4 — Far Zone**
- Delivery Fee: 80 Birr
- Coverage: Kolfe Keranio, Nifas Silk-Lafto
- Estimated Delivery: Standard timeline

**Zone 5 — Outer Zone**
- Delivery Fee: 100 Birr
- Coverage: Akaki-Kality, Lemi Kura
- Estimated Delivery: Standard timeline (may occasionally require additional day for remote areas)

#### Free Delivery Threshold

Orders with a subtotal of **5,000 Birr or more** qualify for **free delivery** to any location within Addis Ababa. This threshold applies to the order subtotal before delivery fees are added.

**Example:**
- Order subtotal: 4,500 Birr → Delivery fee applies based on zone
- Order subtotal: 5,000 Birr → Free delivery to any address in Addis Ababa
- Order subtotal: 8,000 Birr → Free delivery to any address in Addis Ababa

#### Pickup Option

Customers who prefer to collect their orders directly from our workshop can select the **Office Pickup** option during checkout. This option carries **zero delivery fees** and allows you to pick up your completed order at:

**PrintOnline Workshop**  
Pana Promotion PLC  
Bole Sub City, Woreda 03  
Wakero Building  
Addis Ababa, Ethiopia  
Phone: +251911005255

You will receive a notification when your order is ready for pickup.

#### Where You'll See This

- **Checkout page:** Delivery fee displayed in order summary
- **Order confirmation:** Delivery fee shown in payment breakdown
- **Account orders:** Delivery fee visible in order details

### 3.2 Quantity-Based Delivery Discounts

**Category:** E-Commerce Enhancement  
**Impact Level:** Medium — Benefits customers ordering in bulk  
**User-Facing Change:** Larger orders receive discounted or free delivery

#### What Changed

In addition to location-based pricing, Version 3.5 introduces a **quantity-based discount system** for delivery fees. Customers ordering larger quantities of products will benefit from reduced delivery costs, with orders of 51 or more items receiving **completely free delivery** regardless of order value or delivery location.

#### How It Works

The system calculates the total number of items in your cart and applies the appropriate discount tier to your delivery fee:

| Total Items in Order | Delivery Discount | Your Delivery Fee |
|---------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 1–5 items | No discount (standard) | Full zone fee |
| 6–10 items | 20% off delivery | 80% of zone fee |
| 11–20 items | 40% off delivery | 60% of zone fee |
| 21–50 items | 60% off delivery | 40% of zone fee |
| 51+ items | **100% free delivery** | 0 Birr |

#### Combined Benefits

Quantity discounts **stack with** the free delivery threshold. This means you can qualify for free delivery through either:
1. **Value-based:** Order subtotal ≥ 5,000 Birr
2. **Quantity-based:** Order contains 51+ items

Whichever threshold you reach first applies, ensuring maximum savings for your business.

#### Example Scenarios

**Scenario A — Small Order, Same Zone**
- Items: 3 business card packs (Bole delivery)
- Zone fee: 50 Birr
- Quantity tier: 1-5 items (no discount)
- **Final delivery fee: 50 Birr**

**Scenario B — Medium Bulk Order**
- Items: 12 banner orders (Kirkos delivery)
- Zone fee: 60 Birr
- Quantity tier: 11-20 items (40% off)
- **Final delivery fee: 36 Birr**

**Scenario C — Large Wholesale Order**
- Items: 60 t-shirt orders (Akaki delivery)
- Zone fee: 100 Birr
- Quantity tier: 51+ items (free delivery)
- **Final delivery fee: 0 Birr**

### 3.3 Enhanced Order Status Tracking

**Category:** User Experience Enhancement  
**Impact Level:** High — Affects all customers with active orders  
**User-Facing Change:** More detailed and accurate order status information

#### What Changed

The order status tracking system has been completely redesigned to provide customers with a clearer, more detailed view of where their order stands in the production and delivery pipeline. The new system uses a **five-stage milestone tracker** with visual indicators and descriptive status messages.

#### Order Status Pipeline

Your order moves through the following stages:

**Stage 1: Order Received**
- Your order has been placed and payment confirmed
- The PrintOnline team has received your order details
- Design files (if uploaded) are being reviewed

**Stage 2: Design Review**
- Our design team is reviewing your submitted files
- We're checking file quality, dimensions, and print readiness
- If you selected "Hire a Designer," our designer is working on your project
- You may receive messages requesting clarification or file adjustments

**Stage 3: Approved for Production**
- Your design files have been approved
- Your order has been scheduled for printing
- Materials and equipment are being prepared

**Stage 4: In Production**
- Your order is currently being printed/produced
- Quality checks are being performed throughout the process
- This is typically the longest stage depending on order complexity

**Stage 5: Ready for Delivery/Pickup**
- Your order has been completed
- Final quality inspection passed
- Ready for delivery to your address or pickup at our workshop
- You will receive a notification with delivery/pickup details

#### Status Indicators

Each stage uses visual indicators to show progress:
- ✅ **Completed** (green checkmark) — Stage finished successfully
- 🔄 **In Progress** (pulsing indicator) — Currently active stage
- ⏳ **Pending** (gray indicator) — Upcoming stage

#### Where You'll See This

- **Order confirmation page:** Full status tracker displayed
- **Account dashboard:** Order status badges on order list
- **Order detail page:** Detailed status tracker with timestamps
- **Email notifications:** Status update emails sent at each transition

**Order History**

**Step 1: Request Deletion**
1. Navigate to Account Settings
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Review the warning information carefully
4. Enter your password to confirm

- Order history is archived (for business records)

The payment receipt system has been completely redesigned to ensure receipts are **always available** after a successful payment. Previously, some customers experienced issues where the receipt would briefly appear and then disappear after completing payment through the Chapa hosted checkout. **Order Items**
- Product name and description

**Payment Summary**

- Delivery fee

### 3.10 Pending Payment Recovery System

**Category:** Payment Experience Enhancement  
**Impact Level:** Medium — Helps customers recover from payment failures  
**User-Facing Change:** Automatic notifications and retry options for failed payments

#### What Changed

Version 3.5 introduces a **pending payment recovery system** that helps customers who experience payment failures. When a payment attempt fails or is interrupted, the system automatically saves the order, notifies the customer via email, and provides easy options to retry the payment.

#### How It Works

**Payment Attempt**
1. You initiate a checkout and are redirected to Chapa's payment page
2. If the payment fails, times out, or is cancelled:
   - Your order is saved with "Pending Payment" status
   - An email notification is sent to your registered email
   - The order appears in your account with payment pending

**Recovery Options**
- **Email Notification:** Contains order details and a "Retry Payment" button
- **Account Dashboard:** Shows pending orders with easy retry option
- **Direct Link:** Email contains a direct link to complete payment

#### What's Included in the Email

When a payment fails, you'll receive an email containing:
- Order summary (items, quantities, prices)
- Total amount due
- Reason for payment failure (if available)
- Direct link to retry payment
- Contact information for support
- Order number for reference

#### Where You'll See Pending Orders

**Account Dashboard**
- Pending payment orders marked with a distinct indicator
- Clear "Complete Payment" button on each pending order
- Order details accessible for review before retrying

**Order History**
- Pending orders visible alongside completed orders
- Status clearly indicates "Awaiting Payment"
- Easy access to retry payment from order detail page

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Delivery Pricing

**Q: Why did my delivery fee change from what I'm used to?**
A: Version 3.5 introduces location-based delivery pricing. Your delivery fee is now calculated based on the distance between your delivery address and our headquarters in Bole. This ensures fair pricing that reflects actual delivery costs.

**Q: How can I get free delivery?**
A: You can qualify for free delivery in two ways: (1) Place an order with a subtotal of 5,000 Birr or more, or (2) Order 51 or more items in a single order. Whichever threshold you reach first applies.

**Q: What if my sub-city isn't listed?**
A: Please contact our support team and we'll add your area to our delivery network. We're continuously expanding our coverage.

**Q: Can I change my delivery method after placing an order?**
A: Please contact us via the messaging system as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate changes before your order enters production.

### Messaging System

**Q: How quickly will I receive a response to my message?**
A: Our team typically responds within 1-2 hours during business hours (Monday-Saturday, 8:30 AM - 6:00 PM EAT). Messages sent outside business hours will be addressed the next business day.

**Q: Can I send files through the messaging system?**
A: File attachments are not yet supported in the messaging system. Please use the design file upload feature during product configuration, or contact us directly for file sharing needs.

**Q: What if I don't see my message response?**
A: Messages update in real-time. If you don't see a response, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, please contact us through alternative channels.

### Account Management

**Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?**
A: Yes, you have a 30-day recovery window after requesting account deletion. Contact our support team within this period to restore your account.

**Q: What happens to my pending orders if I delete my account?**
A: Pending orders will continue to be processed and delivered. You'll receive email updates about order status even after account deactivation.

**Q: Will I still receive order notifications after deleting my account?**
A: Yes, order-related notifications (status updates, delivery confirmations) will continue to be sent to your registered email address.

### Payments & Receipts

**Q: Why does my receipt sometimes not show immediately after payment?**
A: In Version 3.5, we've fixed this issue. Receipts now display immediately from our database after successful payment verification, rather than relying on the payment gateway's hosted page.

**Q: Can I get a PDF receipt for my order?**
A: Currently, receipts are displayed in HTML format which can be printed or saved as a PDF using your browser's print function. PDF receipt generation is planned for a future update.

**Q: What should I do if my payment failed?**
A: You'll receive an email notification with details and a retry link. You can also view pending payments in your account dashboard and retry from there.

### Designer Service

**Q: How do I know the designer fee for a specific product?**
A: The designer fee is displayed on each product's detail page and during checkout when you select the "Hire a Designer" option.

**Q: What's included in the designer service?**
A: The designer service includes initial design creation, up to 3 revision rounds, final print-ready file delivery, and design consultation through our messaging system.

**Q: Can I communicate with the designer directly?**
A: Yes, use the messaging system linked to your order to discuss design requirements, provide feedback, and request revisions.
