# PrintOnline Ethiopia — Version 3.8 Summary

**Date:** June 2026

---

## What's New in This Update

Version 3.8 is a **reliability and polish** release. Your customers will notice faster, more professional emails and a smoother sign-in experience. The team will benefit from fewer support requests and clearer notifications. All existing features, pricing, and the checkout process remain unchanged.

---

### 1. Faster, More Reliable Email Delivery

**Before:** Customer emails (order confirmations, password resets, account verifications) sometimes arrived late or got lost in spam folders.

**Now:** All customer emails are delivered quickly and reliably, usually within seconds.

**What this means for the business:**
- Customers receive confirmations, invoices, and updates on time
- Password reset emails arrive when customers need them
- Order confirmations build trust at the moment of purchase

---

### 2. Login With Your Google Account

Customers can now sign in with one click using their Google account.

- **For returning customers:** One-click sign-in, no password to remember
- **For new customers:** Create an account instantly using their Google profile
- **Always available:** Email and password sign-in still works as before

**Result:** Fewer abandoned carts and support requests from customers who forgot their passwords.

---

### 3. Better Looking Emails

All customer emails now have a professional, branded design.

- Your company logo and colors in every email
- Clean, mobile-friendly layout that looks great on phones and computers
- Clear call-to-action buttons (e.g., "View Order," "Reset Password," "Verify Email")
- Consistent branding across every email type

**What this means:** Customers immediately recognize emails as official communications from PrintOnline, building trust and reducing confusion.

---

### 4. Account Verification Made Simple

When new customers sign up, they receive a verification email.

- One click to verify and start ordering
- Reduces fake accounts and spam registrations
- Smooth, automatic sign-in after verification — no extra steps

**Benefit:** A cleaner customer base and fewer support tickets about account issues.

---

### 5. Password Recovery That Actually Works

Customers who forget their password can now reset it themselves in minutes.

- Customer clicks "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Receives a secure link via email
- Sets a new password and signs in

**Result:** No more "contact support" for password issues. Customers help themselves 24/7.

---

### 6. Contact Form Now Works Properly

The "Get In Touch" form on the website now reliably delivers messages to the team inbox.

- Customers can ask questions, request quotes, or report issues
- The team receives inquiries directly in the `order@printonline.et` inbox
- The email address displayed on the contact page matches the inbox that actually receives messages

**Benefit:** Fewer lost leads and faster response times to customer inquiries.

---

### 7. Easier for Your Team

The team now gets automatic notifications for important events.

- **New orders:** Admin receives an email with full order details as soon as payment is confirmed
- **Order updates:** When the team changes an order status (e.g., "Under Review," "In Production," "Ready for Delivery"), the customer is automatically notified
- **Payment issues:** If a payment fails, the customer receives a friendly notification with next steps

**Result:** Less manual communication, fewer missed updates, and happier customers.

---

### 8. Behind the Scenes Improvements

The system now self-checks for configuration issues before going live.

- Catches common setup problems early
- Prevents issues that previously required manual fixes
- Ensures the website is always ready for customers

---

## What Stays the Same

- Checkout process
- Order management
- Product pages
- Customer accounts
- Pricing
- Delivery fees
- All previous features (homepage editor, testimonials, deals, quantity configuration, image reordering, cookie banner, social login, product activation toggle, etc.)

---

## Infrastructure Note

Our previous email delivery method had limitations with our hosting provider. We've upgraded to a professional email delivery service that ensures:

- **Faster delivery** (usually within seconds)
- **Higher reliability** (no more lost emails)
- **Better protection against spam filters**
- **Professional templates** for all customer communications

**Important:** Our main email inbox (`order@printonline.et`) is still hosted with EthioTel for receiving email. The improvement is in how we **send** emails to customers. Occasional delays on the receiving side are infrastructure-related and outside our control.

**What the team should do:**
- Outgoing emails to customers are 100% reliable
- Check the `order@printonline.et` inbox at least twice daily
- For time-sensitive customer communication, follow up by phone (see contact page)
- Migrating to a more reliable receiving provider is planned for a future update
